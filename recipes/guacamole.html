Ingredients

  • 2-4 avocados
  • lime juice
  • optional extras: chop up and add for flavor & texture

Steps

  1. Remove the outer skin of the avocados and the pits.
  2. Put the avocados fruit in a bowl.
  3. Mash up the avocados.
  4. Add lime juice to taste.
  5. Eat it up, you're done!
