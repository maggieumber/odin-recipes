PB and Chocolate Smoothie

Description:

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk or water
  • 2 scoops peanut butter
  • 4 frozen bananas
  • 1 teaspoon chocolate powder
  • (optional) chopped veggie

Steps

  1. Pour milk or water in the blender.
  2. Drop in the peanut butter.
  3. Add the frozen bananas.
  4. Sprinkle in the chocolate powder.
  5. For those health points, add a chopped veggie!
  6. Blend it all up.
