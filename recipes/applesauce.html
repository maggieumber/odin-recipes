Ingredients

  • apples (3+)
  • water (optional)
  • cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sugar

Steps

  1. Peel the apples.
  2. Chop up the apples into small chunks.
  3. Throw the chunks into a pan.
  4. Add water to the pan (optional).
  5. Layer cinnamon on top of the apples.
  6. Sprinkle the sugar on top of everything.
  7. Let simmer on the stove.
  8. When the chunks are golden or the water is gone, it's done!
